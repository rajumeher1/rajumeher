About Me

I am a dynamic finance professional with over a decade of rich experience in the banking sector, specializing in operations, credit risk, and management. My journey has been marked by a constant drive to evolve, leading me to blend my deep financial expertise with emerging technologies in data science, machine learning, and generative AI. This dual-lens perspective allows me to approach complex business challenges with both financial intuition and data-driven precision.

During my tenure at Bank of Baroda, I held diverse leadership roles, from Branch Manager to MSME Credit Manager. I was responsible for driving operational efficiency, ensuring regulatory compliance, and leading teams to deliver exceptional customer service. I took great pride in optimizing loan processing workflows and implementing robust risk mitigation strategies that significantly improved portfolio quality and performance.

My pivot to data science in 2022 was a natural progression, driven by the belief that data is the new currency of the financial industry. Through my internship at Innomatics Research Lab, I honed my skills in predictive modeling and built AI-powered applications that streamlined customer service and enhanced decision-making. I've become proficient in Python, SQL, NLP, and various machine learning frameworks, using these tools to solve real-world business problems.

Currently, as a Banking Operations Consultant at Gieom Business Solutions, I'm at the forefront of digital transformation. My work involves moving from traditional Standard Operating Procedures to dynamic Value Stream Operating Procedures. This role allows me to leverage my extensive banking knowledge and new technical skills to help financial institutions become more agile and scalable. I am passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful change in fintech, risk analytics, and AI product development.
